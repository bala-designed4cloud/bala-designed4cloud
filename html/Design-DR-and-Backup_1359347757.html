WebSite : Design DR and Backup

Created by Kanesamoorthy Balakumar, last modified on Mar 01, 2022

The entire engineering discipline evolved based on thousands of disasters and cloud engineering is not exemption. If you have chosen to move your IT assets to cloud you are indirectly binding the principle that weather or not a disaster would strike but it’s a matter of when a disaster would strike?

Yes, in cloud we must architect our solutions to anticipate disaster anytime.

When it strikes:

  • how can we minimise the data loss (RPO) if not completely prevent it

  • how soon we can restore the services (RTO)

All these must be part of your service management BAU processes.

We have extensive experience in automating the entire DR process in and across Cloud, providing backup services using partner solutions and cloud native services and successfully transitioning the same to the service management.

We architect and design DR and backup strategies that aligns with your organisation’s business and compliance requirements that includes but not limited to:

  • Backup and Restore

  • Pilot Light

  • Warm Standby

  • Active - Active

For more details refer https://docs.aws.amazon.com/whitepapers/latest/disaster-recovery-workloads-on-aws/disaster-recovery-options-in-the-cloud.html
