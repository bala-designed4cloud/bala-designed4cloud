Created by Kanesamoorthy Balakumar, last modified on Feb 05, 2022

Platform for each customer may have a different meaning and from customer point of view, all capabilities available in an enterprise platform should be designed with end to end service delivery in mind and should be integrated with the overall service management system and processes.

The overall platform architecture involves, but not limited to:

  • Network Architecture and Design

  • Identity and Access Management

  • Compute Standard Operating Environment (SOE)

  • CI/CD Tooling

  • Cloud Landing Zone Design
