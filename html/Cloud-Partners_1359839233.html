WebSite : Cloud Partners

Created by Kanesamoorthy Balakumar, last modified on Feb 05, 2022

We have partnered with the leading Cloud Providers, working with other Cloud Provider who are getting popular in the market and open to work with any other Cloud Provider as required by our customer.

We are 100% neutral on the selection of Cloud Provider, but we do provide expert advise and recommendation to help the customer’s make choices based on their requirement, internal skills and experience.

But in terms of deciding which Cloud Provider is the final decision is belong our Customer and regardless of our Customers decision, we respect it, work with any Cloud Provider and delivery the business outcome that customer is expected from us.

The key for our success is the sustainability aspect of our customer and how the Cloud Transition can enable for further growth that can be supported purely by their internal staff and existing suppliers without adding new staff or suppliers.

Therefore our key guiding principle is to make sure of customer’s domain expertise, internal staff, enable their internal staff, empower them and supplement with existing suppliers, so towards the end of reach milestone and ultimate destination, the transition become seamless experience for our customer.
