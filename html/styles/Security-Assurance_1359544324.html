We provide services ranging from security risk identification, management and continuous assurance services to https://www.govt.nz/ , NZ Financial Institutions, large enterprises, small and medium businesses as well as startups.

NZ Government

We provide Security Assurance Services to meet

  • New Zealand Information Security Manual

  • https://protectivesecurity.govt.nz/

The services provided to government ranging security procurement of new services and vendors, security architecture, customised security framework to comply with above frameworks, assessment and implementation.

Financial Institutions

Based on our experience, for a successful adoption of cloud in any financial institution, we should get security right from start and always.

There will always be custom frameworks that required to be designed and developed for most of the financial institutions, but our experience in the following frameworks helps us and the client to accelerate the services.

  • https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/

  • https://us.aicpa.org/interestareas/frc/assuranceadvisoryservices/aicpasoc3report.html

  • https://www.iso.org/standards.html

  • https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework

It’s also critical to service transition the security operations with continuous assurance enabled to the internal or contracted security operations team, we take them in a journey right from the start so there are no surprises close to go-live. We don’t believe in compromising security controls for a small benefit of project milestones, but we look at business milestone as a whole with the right level of security risk to enable the business by meeting their expectations.

  • Designing Processes to manage

  • Designing and Implementing solution for continuous assurance.

Security for any entity

We recognise depends on where you are and the exposure of your business, the security risk posture will be different and the amount of effort on architecting, designing and implementing security controls needed to be customised based on the security risk posture of each entity.

We adopt and embrace the policy of security is everyone’s business, so we take our customers on the security journey as they travel with us on the cloud journey in a sustainable manner.
