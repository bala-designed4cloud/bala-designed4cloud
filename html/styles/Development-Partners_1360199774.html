Even in this modern world, with all out of the box SaaS solutions for pretty much all solutions, there is always requirement to develop custom applications with the latest cloud technologies making use of cloud native principles.

The applications designed and developed for cloud is scalable almost unlimited, reliable, resilient, secure and only pay for when you use.

This provides great opportunity as well as flexibility to develop custom cloud native applications over making use of SaaS solutions.

In order to develop applications at scale, we acknowledged the demand for committed development resources, project management and testing capability so we engaged and made arrangement with our development partners to carry out any such small to large custom application development and delivery.

As we go will be working with our development partners to expand our development capabilities as needed.
