WebSite : Well Architected Services

Created by Kanesamoorthy Balakumar, last modified on Mar 01, 2022

Since it’s first launch back in 2012, AWS Well-Architected Framework has become the defacto industry framework for architecting cloud platforms and cloud workloads.

Acknowledging it’s imminent need for any successful cloud adoption, Microsoft launched the same 8 years after it’s competitor AWS, https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/introducing-the-microsoft-azure-wellarchitected-framework/ .

The framework was composed on five pillars and lately AWS introduced sustainability pillar as the sixth pillar, which provides several architecture principles, guidance and standards on what to do and especially what not to do.

The weight of each of the pillars varies across the type of industry, location, size and maturity. Therefore it’s important to understand the business, technology and people and process landscapes before applying the Well Architected Framework and performing review on the same.

We have extensive experience in:

  • adopting, applying and reviewing architecture

  • designing using Well Architected Framework across several customers

  • improving the security posture

  • increasing the value for money

  • enabling operational excellence.
